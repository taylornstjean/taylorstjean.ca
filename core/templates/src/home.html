About Me

I am a first-year Physics doctoral student with an intended focus in computation and experimental high-energy astrophysics at the University of Maryland, College Park. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Comprehensive Physics and Astronomy obtained at the University of Washington in Spring 2024. I graduated with Astronomy departmental honors.

My college career began at Berklee College of Music where I studied Music Production and Engineering. During my second semester, I realized music was more of a hobby than a career path, and I made the decision to leave and pursue my lifelong passion for Physics and Astronomy. I began attending North Seattle College in Spring 2021, graduating in the Spring of the next year with an Associate of Science. I transferred to the University of Washington the following Fall where I earned my Bachelor's degree.
