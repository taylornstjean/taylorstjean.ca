About Me

I was born in Seattle, WA where I spent most of my life. I graduated from Roosevelt High School in 2020, earned an Associate of Science degree from North Seattle College in 2022, and obtained a Bachelor of Science in Physics and Astronomy in 2024 from the University of Washington.

I am a first-year Physics doctoral student at the University of Maryland, College Park. My research interest is focused on high-energy astrophysics, specifically the study of high-energy particles originating from various phenomena such as gamma-ray bursts, supernovae, and black holes.
